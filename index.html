Who am I? A current junior front-end developer, who wants to evolve professionally.
I consider myself a person with a desire to learn new things every day, passionate, determined, disciplined and with a hunger for knowledge.
I am tech and science fan, open to new things, friendly and understanding, a good listener and a good teammate as well an active and hardworking person.

How did I come to want to work in IT? In my past, I studied computer mathematics at the “Lucian Blaga High School” of in Cluj-Napoca. I liked working on the computer with the software since I was a child, I even made a game server and its organization together with my friends.
I was a gaming fan since I was a child. Later in and after high school I made the plan and organized a website for product sales.

In the future I plan to make a website and application for patients and athletes. After high school I learned and practiced the medical and sports field, but it didn't last long and I chose to re-profile myself because I felt that I was losing my potential and I was not satisfied enough.

For me, the tech field is a domain with an exceptional development potential, in terms of salary and others. I manage to be able to continue my other plans that I have (personal as well as professional).
I want to be part of this united and friendly community and to participate in the development of the field and the community.

I'm looking for a job which always have something new to learn and to be able to progress from several points of view:
-income, professional, intellectual, to know
-collaborate remotely with as many colleagues and clients and from as many cultures and countries as possible, plus the possibility to work anywhere, it's amazing.

Why did I choose front-end development? I started with C and C ++ languages, but when I started learning front-end development, I liked that I could immediately notice every change in the code.
I could have a direct impact on users as quickly as possible.
I can be creative and I can to apply the personal pleasure of delivering information, services or products to potential customers in a way that is as pleasant, well-structured and easy to use as possible.

Why would I be a good colleague? I consider myself an organized, analytical and logical person.
I like to organize my ideas, tasks, plans, both for a project and outside of it.
I like to come up with new ideas, to analyze the aspects of a problem, task or project.
I consider that I can cope with stress and be an understanding, helpful and friendly colleague, being able to mule on several types of teams.

What hobbies do I have? The main hobbies are reading and searching for information in the tech field, science, fascinating facts, economics and sports.
My secondary hobbies are motorcycling, numismatics, biology and reading philosophy, personal development, history and politics.
